# AI Assistant Setup — Kick-Off Prompt
# Paste this into a new chat inside your ChatGPT Project to begin setup.

---

```
You're going to help me set up a personalised AI assistant. Start by asking me this one question, then follow the instructions below based on my answer.

**Ask me:**
"Are you primarily:
A) In a technical role — engineer, developer, or integration specialist. You want an AI that knows your systems, your documentation, and your technical work.
B) Looking for a personal advisor and coach — you want an AI that knows you, your goals, and helps you grow professionally and personally.

Which fits you best?"

---

**If they answer A (Technical):**

Tell them: "Great. I'm going to walk you through a technical setup. This takes about 20–30 minutes and you only do it once."

Then fetch and follow the onboarding prompts from:
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1corey-f/ai-set-up/main/technical-team/onboarding-prompts.md

If you can't fetch the URL, tell the user: "Open this link, click Raw, copy everything, and paste it back to me: https://github.com/1corey-f/ai-set-up/blob/main/technical-team/onboarding-prompts.md — then we'll work through it together."

---

**If they answer B (Advisor/Coach):**

Tell them: "Great. I'm going to walk you through a personal advisor setup. This takes about 30–40 minutes and you only do it once. Be honest with your answers — the more specific you are, the more useful this will be."

Then fetch and follow the onboarding prompts from:
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1corey-f/ai-set-up/main/advisor-coach/onboarding-prompts.md

If you can't fetch the URL, tell the user: "Open this link, click Raw, copy everything, and paste it back to me: https://github.com/1corey-f/ai-set-up/blob/main/advisor-coach/onboarding-prompts.md — then we'll work through it together."

---

Work through the prompts one at a time. Don't dump all the questions at once. At the end, generate the output files and tell the user exactly what to save and where to upload them.
```
